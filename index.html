FAQs

We'd rather you ask than wonder —
here are the common ones.

Do I need to be on Xero?

Not necessarily — we work with Xero and MYOB. If you're on something else, we'll help you figure out the best fit and get you set up properly.

How much of my time will this take?

Very little once we're up and running. Onboarding takes more time upfront, but after that you're looking at maybe 30 minutes a month — reviewing reports or answering the odd question.

Do you only work with Australian businesses?

Primarily yes — our expertise is in Australian compliance, and we work exclusively with Australian businesses.

What if my books are a bit of a mess?

That's where we do some of our best work. Catch-up bookkeeping and cleanup jobs are part of the service — we'll get you back on track before moving to ongoing support.

How does the staffing service work?

We source, vet, and place a remote finance professional for your business. They report to you directly, but we handle coordination, timesheet oversight, and quality management.

How is Resync different from a bigger firm?

We're boutique on purpose. We grow by staying focused. You work with Reyz — directly, consistently, and with someone who actually knows what's going on in your business.

Ready to get started?
